Find a Physician

Physicians are responsible for informing the College when there is a change to their practice information, such as whether or not they are accepting new patients.

If a physician has been the subject of disciplinary action by the College since January 2000, this is noted on the Regulatory Action tab on the physician's profile. Inquiries regarding disciplinary action prior to that date should be directed to the College.
